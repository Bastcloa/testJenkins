These tables provide a '''comparison of [[netbook]]s'''.

Aspects of netbooks that should be considered:
* Mouse layout that is used. [[Touchpad]] with 2-buttons below, or touchpad with buttons on each side. The latter may make it hard with some operations needing simultaneous presses<!--formulation?-->.
* Battery capacity and operating time.
* Weight and size. The original concept was below {{nowrap|1 kg}} but some manufacturers tend toward 2&nbsp;kg (4.4&nbsp;lb).
* Noise from CPU fan.
* Driver availability for the built-in hardware.
* [[Operating system]] choice.
* Presence of built-in [[High-Speed Downlink Packet Access|HSDPA]], etc., may help to avoid USB dongles.

This is a '''comparison of [[Macintosh]] models''', produced by [[Apple Inc.]] This list encompasses current models only.

==Market matrix==
Apple follows a product matrix of having a model of computer for both consumers and professionals, in both desktop and portable configurations. This provides a useful background as to the intended market for each Macintosh product.

==Specifications==
'''Head-notes'''<br>
''Pricing''
*<sup>1</sup> Prices in Euros may vary from country to country; the price for [[France]] was used as a rough guide.
*<sup>2</sup> Academic pricing differs in each country and upon where you buy it from. Prices shown in the table below are from the UK online education store. Buying from an Apple retail store typically offers a larger academic discount as they can verify your enrollment by a physical student identification with a picture. For example, the black MacBook from the online education store sells for £58 less than retail, however in the Apple retail store it is sold for £132 less than retail with proper student University identification.
''Hardware''
*<sup>3</sup> Combo means a Combo Drive, which includes a CD reader, [[CD-RW|writer]] and [[DVD]] reader; a [[SuperDrive]] can also write and rewrite DVDs.
*<sup>5</sup> Every current Macintosh uses [[DDR3 SDRAM]] memory. Dedicated graphics cards in Macs use the [[PCI Express]] bus.

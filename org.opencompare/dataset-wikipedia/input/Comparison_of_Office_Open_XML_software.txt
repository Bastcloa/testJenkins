{{Office Open XML}}

The '''Office Open XML''' format ('''OOXML'''), is an [[open format|open]] and [[free file format|free]] [[document file format]] for saving and exchanging editable office documents such as text documents (including memos, reports, and books), [[spreadsheet]]s, [[chart]]s, and presentations.<ref>http://www.ecma-international.org/activities/General/presentingecma.ppt</ref><ref>[http://www.ecma-international.org/publications/index.html Ecma formal publications]</ref><ref>[http://www.xmlopen.org/ooxml-wiki/resources/BSI_OOXML_2007_05.pdf Microsoft PowerPoint - BSI OOXML v1b.ppt]</ref><ref>http://www.mass.gov/Aitd/docs/policies_standards/etrmv4dot0/etrmv4dot0information.rtf</ref><ref>http://kikaku.itscj.ipsj.or.jp/sc34/open/1026.pdf</ref><ref>[http://www.techtree.com/India/News/ISO_puts_Microsofts_OOXML_Standard_on_Hold/551-90178-580.html Techtree.com India > News > Software > ISO puts Microsoft's OOXML Standard on Hold]</ref>

The following tables list applications supporting a version of the Office Open XML standard (ECMA-376 and ISO/IEC 29500:2008).
